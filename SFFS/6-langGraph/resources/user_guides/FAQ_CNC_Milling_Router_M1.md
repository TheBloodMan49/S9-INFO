# Technical Guide - CNC Router M1 (MakerTools)

## Components
* **Spindle:** 2.2kW Water Cooled
* **Collet:** ER20 standard
* **Stepper Drivers:** DM542

## How-To Guides

### Changing the Cutting Tool
1. Ensure Spindle is stopped (0 RPM).
2. Press the **Spindle Lock** button on the head.
3. Use a 22mm wrench to loosen the collet nut.
4. Remove old tool, insert new one.
5. Tighten firmly.

### Extracting a Broken Tool
1. Remove the collet nut completely.
2. Use pliers to extract the broken shank from the collet.
3. Clean the inside of the spindle taper with a cloth before reassembling.

## Safety
* Always wear impact-resistant safety glasses.
* Use a vacuum for chips (no compressed air).
