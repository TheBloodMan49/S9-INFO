# CRYO-GEN COOLING SYSTEM
## Technical Specifications
* **Refrigerant:** R-410A
* **Operating Pressure:** Low side 120 PSI / High side 400 PSI

## Components
* **Compressor:** Scroll Type
* **Main Fan:** Axial Fan-Blade-400mm
* **Piping:** Copper Grade L

## Repair Protocols

### Pipe Welding (Leak Repair)
* **Pre-requisite:** System must be evacuated of refrigerant.
* **Method:** Silver brazing (15% Ag) only.
* **Post-repair:** Vacuum pull required before recharging.

### Fan Replacement
1. Unscrew the 4 bolts holding the front grille.
2. Access the motor shaft.
3. Unscrew the central nut (ATTENTION: REVERSE THREAD - Turn Right to Loosen).
4. Swap the fan blade.

## Safety Alerts
* **R-410A:** Asphyxiation hazard. Ventilate area if a leak occurs.
