*** WELDING STATION SPARK - MANUAL ***

COMPONENTS:
- Torch Handle (TIG/MIG)
- Contact Tip (Copper)
- Wire Feeder Rollers
- Ground Clamp

MAINTENANCE TASKS:

Task 1: Changing the Torch Tip
- Unscrew the gas nozzle.
- Unscrew the copper contact tip.
- Screw in the new tip matching the wire diameter.

Task 2: Cleaning Feeder Rollers
- Open side panel.
- Release the tension arm.
- Wipe rollers with a solvent-free cloth.

SAFETY:
- UV Radiation: Auto-darkening helmet required.
- Wear leather apron and gloves.
