TECHNICAL MANUAL - GENERATOR BACKUP POWER
Model: Gen-X 5000 Diesel

[SERVICEABLE PARTS]
- Engine Oil: 15W40 Synthetic
- Oil Filter: Cartridge Type (Ref: OF-GenX)
- Fuel Filter: Inline clear bowl
- Starter Battery: 12V 60Ah

[MAINTENANCE PROCEDURES]

-- OIL CHANGE --
1. Place a pan under the drain plug.
2. Remove plug, drain warm oil.
3. Unscrew old Oil Filter.
4. Install new filter (hand tight).
5. Refill with 1.5L of 15W40.

-- FUEL FILTER REPLACEMENT --
1. Clamp the fuel line.
2. Unscrew the clear housing.
3. Swap the internal paper cartridge.
4. Prime the fuel bulb before starting.
