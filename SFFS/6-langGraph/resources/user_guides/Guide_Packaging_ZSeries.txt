MODEL: Packaging Unit Z-Series
VENDOR: PackMaster

[SPARE PARTS INVENTORY]
1. Thermal Fuse (Part #TF-200)
2. Heating/Sealing Bar
3. Optical Sensors (Omron E3Z)
4. Main Motherboard (PCB-Z-Series)

[REPLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS]

> REPLACING THE THERMAL FUSE
  - Located under the heating element cover.
  - Cut the old fuse wires.
  - Crimp the new fuse in place (Do not solder, heat will melt solder).

> CLEANING OPTICAL SENSORS
  - Locate the black sensors on the paper path rails.
  - Use a dry cotton swab or compressed air to remove dust.
  - Do not use alcohol on the plastic lens.

[SAFETY]
- High Temperature: Wait 15 mins after shutdown before touching the sealing bar.
